# NIST SP 800-30 Rev. 1 - Guide for Conducting Risk Assessments

## Risk Assessment Process

1. **Prepare for Assessment** - Establish context and objectives
2. **Conduct Assessment** - Identify threats, vulnerabilities, likelihood, impact
3. **Communicate Results** - Share findings with stakeholders
4. **Maintain Assessment** - Update as environment changes

## Risk Calculation

- **Risk = Likelihood x Impact**
- Likelihood: Very Low (1) to Very High (5)
- Impact: Very Low (1) to Very High (5)
- Risk Level: Low (1-6), Moderate (7-12), High (13-25)
